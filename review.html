Frequently Ask Question (FAQ)

What is the best crypto exchange out there?

This does depend on a lot of different factors! The best crypto exchange may differ from person to person - some people are going to value small fees and easy-to-use UI, while others will only use a platform if it has the best possible security measures in place. Whatever the case might be, though, Coinbase and Binance are usually seen as the best alternatives.

Which cryptocurrency exchange is best for beginners??

Reading through various best crypto exchange reviews online, you’re bound to notice that one of the things that most of these exchanges have in common is that they are very simple to use. While some are more straightforward and beginner-friendly than others, you shouldn’t encounter any difficulties with either of the top-rated exchanges. That said, many users believe that Coinbase is one of the simpler exchanges on the current market.

What are the main features of the best crypto exchanges?

The best crypto exchanges are going to have unmatched security features as their main point of reference. Also, such exchanges should possess decent fees, and be easy to use, even for beginner cryptocurrency traders. Exchanges such as Coinbase and Binance do tick all of these points!

What is the difference between a crypto exchange and a brokerage?

In layman’s terms, a cryptocurrency exchange is a place where you meet and exchange cryptocurrencies with another person. The exchange platform (i.e. Binance) acts as a middleman - it connects you (your offer or request) with that other person (the seller or the buyer). With a brokerage, however, there is no “other person” - you come and exchange your crypto coins or fiat money with the platform in question, without the interference of any third party. When considering cryptocurrency exchange rankings, though, both of these types of businesses (exchanges and brokerages) are usually just thrown under the umbrella term - exchange. This is done for the sake of simplicity.
